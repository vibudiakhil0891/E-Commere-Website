About Our Village

Kuppayapalem is a peaceful and culturally rich village in Dakkili Mandal, Tirupati District. Surrounded by lush green fields and friendly people, it holds a deep history of unity, tradition, and agricultural heritage. The villagers take pride in their festivals, local temples, and the simplicity of rural life that defines true happiness.
